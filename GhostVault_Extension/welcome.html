Your Security Matters

🔒 Local Storage: All your data is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB.

🔐 Encryption: Passwords are encrypted with AES-GCM using your master password.

🌐 No Cloud Storage: Your data never leaves your device unless you choose to sync.

🔍 Open Source: Review the code to ensure transparency and security.
