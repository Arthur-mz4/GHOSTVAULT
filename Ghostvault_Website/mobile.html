Notes & limitations

  • Mobile OSes restrict device‑wide scanning; storage scans work on picked files.
  • Tracker blocking is basic due to WebView limitations.
  • Preview quality: expect frequent improvements.

Current focus

  • Improving accuracy for file hashing and VT flows.
  • Better history coverage for link scans.
  • UX refinements and stability.
Is the app free?

Yes. It runs locally on your device. Optional APIs like VirusTotal may have their own plans/limits.

Where is data stored?

Settings and history are stored locally on the device (AsyncStorage). No analytics.

How stable is the preview?

Core flows work, but it’s evolving. Expect rapid updates.
