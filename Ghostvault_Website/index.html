Frequently asked questions

Is GhostVault free?

Yes. It’s a developer‑friendly, locally run toolkit. Some optional APIs (e.g., HIBP, VirusTotal) may require free or paid keys.

Where is my data stored?

On your device: the extension uses IndexedDB and the mobile app uses AsyncStorage. Sensitive items are encrypted client‑side.

Does GhostVault track me?

No. There’s no telemetry or analytics. External calls only occur when you explicitly use breach or reputation lookups.

How stable is the mobile app?

The core flows work in preview (safe browser, link/file scanning), but it’s still evolving. Expect rapid improvements.

How do I uninstall?

Remove the extension from your browser’s extensions page. For mobile, uninstall the app or stop the Expo preview.
